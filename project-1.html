CarPal - The Carpooling App

CarPal is a mobile application designed to make daily commuting more sustainable and cost-efficient. It connects drivers and passengers traveling on similar routes, encouraging eco-friendly shared rides.

Design Process

  • Research

    Surveys & competitor analysis to identify pain points in commuting.

  • Wireframing

    Low-fidelity sketches to plan navigation & structure.

  • UI Design

    Crafted modern, minimal UI in Figma with focus on usability.

Project Gallery

Impact

CarPal simplified carpooling for urban users and contributed to reducing carbon footprint. With a clean design and intuitive flow, it received positive feedback during prototype testing.
